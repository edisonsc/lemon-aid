lemonaid

When life gives you lemons, make lemonade.

Over the past 1.5 years, our daily lives have been profoundly changed by the COVID-19 pandemic. We strongly believe that everyone is needed help and support. And one of the tool to cope with the stress is our Lemonaid social service.

Do you feel stressed?

Tell your story and what you feel during pandemic. Chat with people who can understand you and offer help. You will find that positive mood is a great tool to cope with problems.

Are you ready to help?

If you are that person, who is always there for help and want to cheer up people who suffered from COVID, you are on the right track. Share some jokes or memes, or just chat.

COVID-19 Digital Diary?

Write everything you want, track your simptoms, habits, feelings, or physical activities. Diary will remind you when to take medications, or to call a psychologist.

Medical help online

If you need not only moral support and humour, but also a professional approach, you can easily consult with our specialists.
